Recipe for pesto pasta


Description:

Make your own Italian style pesto pasta by following the below recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Pesto

Steps:

  1. Let water boil, then add pasta and leave in for as long as it needs, usually 5-10 minutes.
  2. When the pasta is done, remove the water from the pot, add pesto and stir.
  3. Put in a plate to enjoy your pesto pasta! Feel free to add cheese and tomatoes.
