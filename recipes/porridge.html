Recipe for Porridge


Description:

Make your own beautiful porridge bowl by following the below recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Milk or Water
  • Anything else you want to add in/on top!

Steps:

  1. Let your oats simmer in a pot with no milk or water to grill them slightly first.
  2. After about 5 minutes of regularly mixing them, add milk or water, about the same volume as the oats.
  3. Set to medium heat and make sure to stir every minute at least. The more the better.
  4. Once your porridge has the texture you want it to have, pour it into a bowl.
  5. Add any ingredients you like and voila, you've made a beautiful porridge bowl!
  6. Ingredients that work well are berries, bananas, peanut butter, nuts, seeds... Get creative!
