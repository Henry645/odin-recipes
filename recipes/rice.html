Recipe for shrimp and egg rice


Description:

Make your own shrimp egg rice by following the below recipe!

Ingredients:

  • Jasmine rice
  • Water
  • Frozen shrimps
  • 1-2 eggs

Steps:

  1. Add rice, water, shrimp and egg in a rice cooker.
  2. Set on the right setting and start the process.
  3. Enjoy your tasty shrimp egg rice!
